OUR VISION

Altar of Holy fire’s vision is to support believers in maintaining deep intimacy with God, fellowship with the brethren, and truly represent Christ to the world in words and actions.

As the priests were commanded in Leviticus 6:12 to keep the fire burning continuously on the altar by adding wood, we are also to keep the fire of God’s love and present burning in our hearts by continual fellowship and deep intimacy with the Holy Spirit, resulting in carrying His manifested presence that enables us to partner with Him to fulfil His divine purposes and plans.
