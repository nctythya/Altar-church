About Us

ALTAR OF HOLY FIRE PRAYER MINISTRIES

The fire shall ever be burning upon the altar Leviticus 6:12-13, Acts 2:3

Our Vision

Altar of Holy fire’s vision is to support believers in maintaining deep intimacy with God, fellowship with the brethren, and truly represent Christ to the world in words and actions.

As the priests were commanded in Leviticus 6:12 to keep the fire burning continuously on the altar by adding wood, we are also to keep the fire of God’s love and present burning in our hearts by continual fellowship and deep intimacy with the Holy Spirit, resulting in carrying His manifested presence that enables us to partner with Him to fulfil His divine purposes and plans.

The Alter on which the fire is to keep burning is the heart of spirit filled believers who are focused on advancing the kingdom of God through disciple making.

When our hearts are continually on fire for God and His divine purposes and plans, together we can carry out the great Commission and great commandment, and truly represent Christ to a world that needs to know Him.

God is calling us to a life of deeper intimacy with himself, so that the continue fire of his love presence can burn in us, to enable us to accomplish his mission and purpose, intimacy birth partnership, Jesus said in John 15:5 that without Him we can do nothing. Read more...

Our Mission Statement

Our mission is to help people know Christ and develop intimacy with him, so they can grow strong in Christ and take that experience to the entire world. Our Ministry is guided by The Great Commandment and The Great Commission.

We reach people and make disciples for Christ, by exposing them to Biblical teachings, personal fellowship with the Holy Spirit, teaching them how to maintain their fire and zeal for God and his kingdom, so they can go out and make disciples for Christ also. "We believe in the great Commission and the great commandment.

The Great Commandment
"Jesus said, 'Love the Lord your God with all your heart...soul...and mind. This is the first and greatest commandment. And the second is like it: Love your Neighbor as yourself. All the Law and Prophets hang on these two commandments.' " Matthew 22:36-40

To "love God with all your heart" is worship.
To "love your Neighbor as yourself" is ministry.
To "make disciples" is evangelism.
To "teach them...all things" is discipleship.

Our Value Statements

We are committed to equipping Christians through biblical teaching and preaching of God’s word.

We are committed to reliance on the Holy Spirit and the word of God for direction, conception, planning and execution of the ministries and activities of this Ministry.

We are committed to small group ministry because it is where most individual transformation, conversion and leadership development takes place.

We are committed to helping people discover their spiritual gifts that they might use them in the building up of the Body of Christ, (the church).

We are committed to all people, without regards to race or age, and we also value diversity.

We are committed to receiving direction from the Holy Spirit that enables creativity and innovation. We are more concerned with following the lead of the Holy Spirit in ministry than with adherence to tradition or popular view.

We are committed to maintaining biblical standard of excellence in all ministries.

We believe that life is meant to be given away and that Christianity exists to share Christ with others. We will develop aptitudes that encourage numerical growth without compromising in any way our integrity or our commitment to biblical truth.

Our Believe

We believe in God the father almighty, maker of heaven and earth.

We believe that Jesus was conceived by the Holy Spirit and born of the Virgin Mary.

We believe in the Divinity of Jesus Christ.

We believe he suffer and died on the cross for the sins of mankind.

We believe He descended into hell and on the third day He rose from the dead.

We believe He ascended into heaven and is at the right hand of God.

We believe that Jesus is coming back.

We believe in the Holy Spirit and his ministry.

We believe in the church as the body of Christ.

We believe in the resurrection and glorification of the saints and everlasting life.

We believe in the old and New Testament as the full counsel of God.
